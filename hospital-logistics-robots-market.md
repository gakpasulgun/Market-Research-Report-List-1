<p><ul><li>Automated Guided Vehicle</li><li>Mobile Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hospital logistics robots can be categorized into Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs) and mobile robots. AGVs follow predefined paths using sensors and magnetic strips for transporting supplies and medications within healthcare facilities. In contrast, mobile robots utilize advanced AI and navigation systems to autonomously navigate complex environments, adapting to obstacles while performing tasks like delivery and sanitation. Both types enhance operational efficiency, reduce human labor, and improve the overall workflow in hospitals, ensuring timely and accurate logistics management.</p></p>
