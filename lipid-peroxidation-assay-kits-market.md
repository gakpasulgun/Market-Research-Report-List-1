<p><p>Lipid Peroxidation Assay Kits are essential for measuring oxidative stress in biological samples. The market is primarily divided into two types: colorimetric and fluorometric kits. Colorimetric assays rely on color change to quantify lipid peroxidation, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, fluorometric assays utilize fluorescence measurements, providing higher sensitivity and precision. Both types facilitate research in fields like biochemistry, toxicology, and pharmacology by enabling scientists to assess oxidative damage and its implications in various diseases.</p></p>
