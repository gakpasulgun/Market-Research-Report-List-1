<p><p>The Commercial Single Phase Electricity Smart Meter market encompasses two main types: IC Card and Non-IC Card meters. IC Card meters utilize integrated circuit cards for prepaid electricity management, allowing users to recharge their meters easily and monitor usage in real time. In contrast, Non-IC Card meters typically operate on postpaid systems, where users receive monthly bills based on consumption. Both types enhance energy management, but they differ in payment methods and consumer convenience, catering to diverse user preferences and needs.</p></p>
