<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Booster Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multiple Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The booster pump market is categorized into two main types: single stage and multiple stage pumps. Single-stage booster pumps are designed to increase fluid pressure with a single impeller, making them ideal for low-pressure applications, such as residential water supply. In contrast, multiple-stage booster pumps feature two or more impellers, allowing for higher pressure increases suitable for industrial applications, high-rise buildings, and long-distance water transportation. The choice between these types depends on the required pressure, flow rate, and application specifics.</p></p>
