<p><p>The thermal imaging camera market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging cameras. Uncooled thermal imaging cameras operate without the need for cooling systems, making them more compact, lightweight, and cost-effective, suitable for various applications like security and surveillance. In contrast, cooled thermal imaging cameras utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance image resolution and sensitivity, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as military, aerospace, and industrial inspections, where detecting minute temperature variations is crucial.</p></p>
