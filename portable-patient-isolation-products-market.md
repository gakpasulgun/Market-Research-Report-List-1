<p><p>The Portable Patient Isolation Products market consists of Negative Pressure and Positive Pressure systems. Negative Pressure systems create a vacuum environment, preventing airborne pathogens from escaping, making them ideal for isolating patients with infectious diseases. In contrast, Positive Pressure systems provide a controlled, clean environment by filtering incoming air, reducing the risk of infection for immunocompromised patients. Both types facilitate safe patient transport while minimizing the risk of cross-contamination in healthcare settings.</p></p>
