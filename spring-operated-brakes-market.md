<p><strong>The Spring Operated Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Spring-operated Brakes</li><li>Electromagnetic Spring-operated Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spring Operated Brakes market is divided into two primary types: Hydraulic Spring-operated Brakes and Electromagnetic Spring-operated Brakes. Hydraulic Spring-operated Brakes use hydraulic pressure to disengage the brake when needed while relying on springs for automatic engagement, ensuring reliable performance in heavy machinery. Electromagnetic Spring-operated Brakes, on the other hand, employ electromagnetic forces to disengage while allowing springs to re-engage when power is lost, making them ideal for applications requiring quick response and fail-safe operation.</p></p>
