<p><p>Geothermal heating and cooling systems utilize the Earth's stable temperature for climate control. Vertical coil installations involve deep vertical boreholes with coils for efficient heat exchange, ideal for smaller lots. Horizontal coil installations spread pipes in shallow trenches, suitable for larger spaces. Pond systems use a body of water for heat exchange, taking advantage of the relatively constant temperatures, making them effective in regions with existing ponds. Each type offers unique benefits depending on land availability and environmental conditions.</p></p>
