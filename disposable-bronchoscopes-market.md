<p><p>The disposable bronchoscopes market is primarily categorized into two types: rigid and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes feature a straight, non-flexible design, providing direct visualization and access for procedures, making them suitable for interventions like biopsies and foreign body removal. In contrast, flexible bronchoscopes are adaptable and can navigate the complex anatomy of the airways, allowing for effective diagnostics and treatment in various medical settings. Both types are gaining traction due to increased infection control and reduced reprocessing concerns.</p></p>
