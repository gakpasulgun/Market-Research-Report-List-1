<p><p>The analog integrated circuits market encompasses various types, including power management circuits and operational amplifiers. Power management circuits regulate and optimize electrical energy usage in devices, crucial for battery efficiency and thermal management. They ensure stable voltage and current levels across systems. Operational amplifiers, on the other hand, amplify electrical signals and are essential in signal processing applications. Their versatility enables applications in filtering, comparators, and analog-to-digital conversions, making them vital components in numerous electronic systems.</p></p>
