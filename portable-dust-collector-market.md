<p><ul><li>Bag</li><li>Cartridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable dust collector market encompasses two primary types: bag collectors and cartridge collectors. Bag collectors use large fabric bags to filter and collect dust particles, making them suitable for heavier dust loads and less frequent maintenance. In contrast, cartridge collectors utilize pleated filters that provide higher filtration efficiency and can trap finer particles, often making them ideal for situations that require cleaner air. Both types cater to various industrial applications, balancing efficiency, cost, and space considerations.</p></p>
