<p><p>The plastic pallet market is primarily categorized into HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) and PP (Polypropylene) segments. HDPE pallets are known for their durability, resistance to chemicals, and ability to withstand heavy loads, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, PP pallets are lighter, more flexible, and offer excellent impact resistance, suitable for transportation and retail environments. Both types are eco-friendly, reusable, and contribute to reducing reliance on traditional wood pallets, enhancing overall supply chain efficiency.</p></p>
