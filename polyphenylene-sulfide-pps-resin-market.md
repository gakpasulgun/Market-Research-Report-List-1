<p><p>The Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) resin market can be categorized into two primary types: linear type and cross-linked type. Linear PPS is characterized by its flexible molecular structure, offering excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications in automotive and electrical industries. In contrast, cross-linked PPS has a rigid, interconnected structure that enhances its mechanical strength and dimensional stability, ideal for high-performance applications such as aerospace and industrial components. Both types serve specific industry needs based on their unique properties.</p></p>
