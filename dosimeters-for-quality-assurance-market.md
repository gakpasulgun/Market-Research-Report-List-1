<p><p>Dosimeters for quality assurance are essential tools used to measure radiation exposure in various settings. TLD (Thermoluminescent Dosimeters) utilize materials that trap energy from radiation; when heated, they release light proportional to the absorbed radiation. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters use similar principles but are stimulated by light to measure radiation levels. Other dosimeters may include electronic or film-based options, catering to specific monitoring needs across industries like healthcare, manufacturing, and environmental safety.</p></p>
