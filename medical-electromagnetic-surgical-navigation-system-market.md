<p><p>The Medical Electromagnetic Surgical Navigation System market is primarily divided into two types: Hybrid Navigation and Electromagnetic Navigation. Hybrid Navigation combines optical and electromagnetic technologies, offering enhanced precision and visualization during surgical procedures. This system allows for real-time tracking and adaptability to various surgical environments. Electromagnetic Navigation, on the other hand, uses electromagnetic fields to locate instruments within the body, providing accurate positioning without reliance on line-of-sight, making it suitable for complex and minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
