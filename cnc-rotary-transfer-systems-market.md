<p><p>CNC rotary transfer systems are classified into vertical axis and horizontal axis types, each offering distinct operational advantages. Vertical axis systems optimize space by allowing vertical tool movement, enhancing efficiency in processes requiring height adjustments. Conversely, horizontal axis systems facilitate easy access and line integration, making them suitable for high-volume production with straightforward loading and unloading. Both systems enhance productivity and precision in machining operations, catering to various industrial applications, from automotive to aerospace manufacturing.</p></p>
