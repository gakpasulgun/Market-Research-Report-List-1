<p><p>The industrial refrigeration systems market encompasses key components such as compressors, condensers, and evaporators. Compressors pressurize refrigerants, increasing temperature and facilitating heat transfer. Condensers then cool and condense the refrigerant back into a liquid state, releasing heat. Evaporators absorb heat from the environment, allowing the refrigerant to evaporate and produce a cooling effect. Together, these components form a closed-loop system essential for various applications, including food preservation, chemical processing, and temperature control in industrial settings.</p></p>
