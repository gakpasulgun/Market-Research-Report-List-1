<p><ul><li>Normal Method</li><li>Biotechnological Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seed and plant breeding market can be categorized into two main types: normal methods and biotechnological methods. Normal methods involve traditional breeding techniques, such as selective breeding and hybridization, to enhance desirable traits in plants over generations. In contrast, biotechnological methods utilize advanced techniques like genetic engineering, molecular markers, and tissue culture to accelerate the development of improved plant varieties. Both approaches aim to increase crop yield, resilience, and nutritional quality, thereby supporting global food security.</p></p>
