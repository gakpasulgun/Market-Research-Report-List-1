<p><p>The freezer bags market is primarily segmented into three types of polyethylene: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE. HDPE (High-density polyethylene) is known for its strength and rigidity, making it suitable for bulk storage. MDPE (Medium-density polyethylene) offers a balance of flexibility and strength, ideal for general freezer use. LDPE (Low-density polyethylene) is highly flexible and impermeable, making it perfect for sealing in moisture and preventing freezer burn. Each type serves distinct consumer needs based on durability and storage characteristics.</p></p>
