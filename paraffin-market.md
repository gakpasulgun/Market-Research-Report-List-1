<p><p>The paraffin market comprises three main types: fully refined, semi-refined, and crude paraffin. Fully refined paraffin is extensively processed, resulting in high purity and clarity, making it ideal for cosmetic and food applications. Semi-refined paraffin contains some impurities and is used in various industrial applications like candles and coatings. Crude paraffin, extracted directly from oil refining, retains natural waxes and oils, primarily serving as a feedstock for further processing or in its raw form in niche applications.</p></p>
