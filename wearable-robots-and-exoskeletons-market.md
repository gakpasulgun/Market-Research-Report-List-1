Full-body exoskeletons support the entire body, enhancing mobility and strength for rehabilitation or industrial use. Upper-body exoskeletons focus on assisting arm and shoulder movements, ideal for reducing fatigue in manual labor. Lower-body exoskeletons primarily aid leg movement, benefiting individuals with mobility impairments or enhancing worker efficiency in physically demanding tasks.</p></p>
