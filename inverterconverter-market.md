<p><p>The inverter/converter market can be categorized into two primary types: inverters and converters. Inverters transform direct current (DC) into alternating current (AC), essential for renewable energy systems and electronics. Converters, on the other hand, adjust voltage levels and can change AC to DC or vice versa, supporting power distribution and management. Both play crucial roles in renewable energy applications, electric vehicles, and industrial systems, driving demand for efficiency and advanced technologies in energy conversion and power management solutions.</p></p>
