<p><strong>The Automotive Powertrain Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ATOC</li><li>ATF</li><li>ITOC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive powertrain cooling system market includes various types designed to manage engine and transmission temperatures. ATOC (Automotive Thermal Oil Cooling) uses thermal oils to regulate heat, while ATF (Automatic Transmission Fluid) specifically cools automatic transmissions. ITOC (Integrated Thermal Oil Cooling) combines engine and transmission cooling in a unified system for efficiency. Additionally, the "Others" category encompasses emerging technologies and specialized solutions like electric vehicle cooling systems, highlighting the market's diversity and innovation in thermal management systems.</p></p>
