Transistor-output optocouplers offer fast switching and are suitable for digital signals. Triac-output optocouplers control AC loads, making them popular in lighting and motor control. Thyristor-output types manage high-power applications, while IC-output optocouplers integrate multiple functions within a single chip for compact designs.
