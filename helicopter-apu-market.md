<p><p>The helicopter Auxiliary Power Unit (APU) market is primarily divided into two types: electric APUs and pneumatic APUs. Electric APUs generate electrical power to support onboard systems and increase efficiency, often offering quieter operation and reduced emissions. In contrast, pneumatic APUs utilize compressed air to start the main engines and provide bleed air for environmental control, ensuring effective performance in various operational conditions. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing helicopter efficiency and overall operational reliability.</p></p>
