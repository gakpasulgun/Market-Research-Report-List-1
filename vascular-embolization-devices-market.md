<p><p>The vascular embolization devices market is primarily categorized into coiling and non-coiling devices. Coiling devices, often used for aneurysm treatment, involve the insertion of small coils into blood vessels to induce clotting and prevent blood flow. Non-coiling devices encompass a range of products like particulate agents, liquid embolics, and balloons, utilized to occlude or obstruct blood vessels for various conditions. This diverse market supports minimally invasive procedures, offering targeted interventions for vascular and tumor-related issues.</p></p>
