<p><ul><li>Flat-flat Type</li><li>Round-flat Type</li><li>Round-round Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foil stamping machine market is categorized into three main types: flat-flat, round-flat, and round-round. </p><p>Flat-flat type machines employ flat dies to apply foil onto flat surfaces, ensuring precision for applications like packaging and labels. Round-flat type machines feature flat dies for stamping on curved surfaces, suitable for objects like bottles and jars. Round-round type machines use circular dies to stamp on cylindrical items, enabling intricate designs on products like tubes and containers. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
