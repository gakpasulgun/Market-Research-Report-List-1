<p><p>Indoor farming encompasses various market types, including hydroponics, aeroponics, aquaponics, soil-based, and hybrid systems. Hydroponics utilizes nutrient-rich water to grow plants without soil, while aeroponics suspends roots in air, misting them with nutrients. Aquaponics combines fish farming with plant cultivation, creating a symbiotic environment. Soil-based systems use traditional soil with controlled conditions, and hybrid systems integrate multiple techniques for efficiency. Other markets may include vertical farming and container farming, enhancing urban agriculture and resource use.</p></p>
