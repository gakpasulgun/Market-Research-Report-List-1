<p><p>A Patent Foramen Ovale (PFO) Closure Device is a medical device used to treat a PFO, which is a small, flap-like opening between the heart's atria that may not close properly after birth. Such openings can lead to complications, including strokes and migraines. PFO closure devices are designed to occlude this opening, typically through a minimally invasive procedure, reducing the risk of associated complications.</p><p>The PFO Closure Device Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing awareness of PFO-related health risks, advancements in medical technologies, and a rise in minimally invasive surgical procedures.
