<p><p>The Dedicated Hybrid Transmission market is primarily split into two types: Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)-based and Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)-based systems. CVT-based hybrids utilize a belt-and-pulley design to provide seamless acceleration and improved fuel efficiency. In contrast, DCT-based hybrids feature two clutches that enable rapid gear shifts, enhancing performance and responsiveness. Both types cater to the growing demand for fuel-efficient, high-performance vehicles, offering distinct advantages in terms of drivability and energy management in hybrid systems.</p></p>
