<p><p>The hydronic underfloor heating component market consists of several key types: heating pipes, manifolds, sensors, and thermostats. Heating pipes circulate warm water beneath the floor, ensuring even heat distribution. Manifolds connect the pipes to the heating source and regulate water flow. Sensors monitor the temperature to optimize comfort and energy efficiency, while thermostats allow users to set desired temperatures and control the heating system automatically. Together, these components create an efficient and effective underfloor heating solution.</p></p>
