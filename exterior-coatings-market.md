<p><strong>The Exterior Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Thinned Coatings for Exterior Wall</li><li>Solvent-Waterd Inorganic Exterior Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exterior coatings market includes solvent-thinned coatings and solvent-watered inorganic coatings for exterior walls. Solvent-thinned coatings are formulated with organic solvents, ensuring quick drying and strong adherence, making them suitable for various substrates and weather resistance. In contrast, solvent-watered inorganic coatings utilize water as a solvent along with inorganic compounds, offering durability, breathability, and environmental benefits. These types emphasize protection and aesthetic appeal in construction and renovation, catering to diverse architectural needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
