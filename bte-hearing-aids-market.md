<p><p>The Behind-The-Ear (BTE) hearing aids market is primarily categorized into two types: Digital Signal Processing (DSP) hearing aids and Simulated Signal Processing hearing aids. DSP hearing aids utilize advanced technology to analyze sound and enhance clarity, providing users with customizable settings for various environments. In contrast, Simulated Signal Processing hearing aids rely on pre-set algorithms to amplify sound without real-time adjustments, offering a more straightforward option for users. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the hearing-impaired community.</p></p>
