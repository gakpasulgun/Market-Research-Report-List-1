<p><p>Automated Vertical Lift Systems (AVLS) are categorized into two main market types: Single Level Delivery and Dual Level Delivery. Single Level Delivery systems facilitate the movement of goods within a single elevation, optimizing space and efficiency for warehouses and retail outlets. In contrast, Dual Level Delivery systems allow for the transportation of items between two distinct vertical levels, enhancing workflow and storage capacity. Both types improve inventory handling, reduce labor costs, and increase throughput in various industrial applications.</p></p>
