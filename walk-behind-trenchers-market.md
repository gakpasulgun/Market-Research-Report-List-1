<p><p>Walk-behind trenchers are essential tools used for creating narrow trenches in various applications, such as plumbing and landscaping. There are two main types: wheel trenchers and chain trenchers. Wheel trenchers operate using a rotating wheel fitted with hardened, serrated blades that cut through the soil, making them efficient for softer ground. Chain trenchers, on the other hand, feature a continuous chain with teeth that dig deeper into harder surfaces, providing greater versatility for various soil conditions and trenching depths.</p></p>
