<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fixed X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market is categorized into two types: Direct Conversion and Indirect Conversion. Direct Conversion detectors use a semiconductor material that converts X-rays directly into electrical signals, providing high image quality and improved sensitivity. In contrast, Indirect Conversion detectors utilize a two-step process, where X-rays are first converted into visible light and then into electrical signals, often resulting in a broader range of applications and cost-effectiveness. Both types are essential for advanced medical imaging technologies.</p></p>
