<p><strong>The Rhinometry Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rhinomanometry System</li><li>Rhinoresistometry Sytem</li><li>Acoustic Rhinometry System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rhinometry systems market comprises three primary types: Rhinomanometry systems measure nasal airflow and resistance during breathing to assess nasal function. Rhinoresistometry systems evaluate the resistance of air passage through the nasal cavity, providing insights into nasal obstruction. Acoustic rhinometry systems use sound waves to analyze the geometrical properties of the nasal cavity, offering a non-invasive method to assess nasal patency and volume. Together, these systems enhance understanding of nasal disorders and help guide clinical interventions.</p></p>
