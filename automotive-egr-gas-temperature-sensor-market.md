<p><strong>The Automotive EGR Gas Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistance Type</li><li>Thermocouples Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) gas temperature sensor market primarily includes resistance type and thermocouple type sensors. Resistance type sensors measure temperature via changes in electrical resistance, providing accurate readings at lower costs, making them popular in budget-friendly applications. In contrast, thermocouples use the thermoelectric effect, offering broader temperature ranges and faster response times, suitable for high-performance and extreme conditions. Both types play vital roles in optimizing engine efficiency and emissions control in modern vehicles.</p></p>
