<p><p>The paper and pulp market comprises various pulp types, each serving distinct purposes. Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp (BSK) is favored for its strength and brightness, commonly used in high-quality paper products. Birch Hardwood Kraft Pulp (BHK) offers good tensile strength and is often utilized in specialty papers and packaging. High Yield Pulp (HYP) is produced through mechanical processing, retaining more lignin, making it cost-effective for lower-grade papers such as newsprint and recycled products, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
