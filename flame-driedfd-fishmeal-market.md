<p><p>The Flame Dried (FD) fishmeal market comprises three main types: Whole Fish Meal, Semi-skimmed Fish Meal, and Defatted Fish Meal. Whole Fish Meal contains the entire fish, retaining all essential nutrients, making it highly nutritious. Semi-skimmed Fish Meal has a reduced fat content while still preserving significant proteins and vitamins. Defatted Fish Meal removes most of the fat, focusing on protein, making it suitable for specific dietary requirements in aquaculture and livestock feed. Each type serves distinct markets based on nutritional needs.</p></p>
