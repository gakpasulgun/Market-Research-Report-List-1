<p><p>Cancer testing kits are categorized based on the specific biomarkers they detect. The FIT test identifies colorectal cancer through stool samples, while the HPV test screens for high-risk human papillomavirus linked to cervical cancer. The PSA test measures prostate-specific antigen for prostate cancer screening. AFP tests detect alpha-fetoprotein levels related to liver cancer, and CEA tests assess carcinoembryonic antigen for colorectal cancer monitoring. Additional tests encompass a range of other biomarkers, facilitating early detection and personalized treatment strategies.</p></p>
