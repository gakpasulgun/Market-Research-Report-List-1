<p><p>The commercial washer market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automatic and fully automatic washers. Semi-automatic washers require manual intervention for loading and unloading clothes, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, making them suitable for smaller businesses. In contrast, fully automatic washers automate the entire washing process, including loading, washing, rinsing, and drying, providing higher efficiency and labor savings. This type is preferred by larger establishments that demand consistent performance and higher capacity for productivity.</p></p>
