<p><p>The laser glass marking market mainly comprises two types: CO2 laser marking and UV laser marking. CO2 laser marking employs a gas laser, ideal for engraving, cutting, and marking various materials, particularly non-metallic substrates like glass. On the other hand, UV laser marking utilizes ultraviolet light for high-precision applications, allowing for minimal thermal impact and the ability to mark delicate surfaces without damage. Both technologies offer unique advantages, catering to different industrial needs and product specifications in the glass marking sector.</p></p>
